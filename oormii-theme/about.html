The story we wear every day

A small atelier in India. A relentless love for craft. Pieces designed to outlive the trend.

Born of Light

A quiet idea, made bold.

Oormii began the way most beautiful things do — quietly. A sketchbook on a grey afternoon. A piece of silver wire bent into a teardrop. The realisation that the best jewellery is worn, not stored.

Today, every Oormii piece is hand-finished by artisans we know by name, from designs we've sketched, prototyped, and rejected dozens of times before they ever reach you. We are unhurried. We are deliberate. We are luminous on purpose.

What We Believe

Three things, fiercely.

◆

Craft over volume

Small batches. Hand-finished edges. Stones set by people, not machines. We'd rather make 50 perfect pieces than 5,000 average ones.

◐

Anti-tarnish, always

A premium rhodium layer, sealed onto every piece, means your jewellery stays luminous through monsoons, makeup, and the passage of years.

⚭

Made to be worn

Comfortable to live in. Light enough to forget. Beautiful enough to remember. Daily-wear silver shouldn't feel like a special occasion.

Milestones

A short
history

2020

A sketchbook on a rainy afternoon

The first Oormii silhouette is sketched — a teardrop pendant. It still hangs in our atelier, framed.

2021

First atelier opens

Three artisans, one bench, twelve designs. The Tear Drop launches and sells out in 48 hours.

2023

Anti-tarnish — perfected

After eighteen months of testing, we lock in our rhodium-coat formulation. Every piece, every batch.

2025

Luxe Edit launches

Our first numbered, limited-edition collection. Sold out in eleven days, restocked twice.

2026

Fifty thousand jewellery boxes opened

More importantly, fifty thousand stories begun. We're just getting started.
