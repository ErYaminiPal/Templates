Questions, gently answered

Everything you might wonder about ordering, sizing, caring for, and returning your Oormii pieces.

How long does shipping take? +

All orders are dispatched within 2–3 business days. Standard delivery typically takes 3–5 days within metros, and 5–8 days for the rest of India. Express delivery is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Is shipping really free? +

Free shipping on all orders above ₹999, anywhere in India. Below that, a flat ₹99 shipping fee applies. International shipping is calculated at checkout and varies by destination.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes — we ship to most countries. Rates and delivery times are calculated at checkout. Note that customs duties, where applicable, are the buyer's responsibility.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a no-questions-asked 7-day return window from delivery. Items must be unworn and in original packaging with all tags. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the return.

How do I initiate a return or exchange? +

Sign in to your Oormii account and click "Return / Exchange" next to the order, or email us at hello@oormiijewels.in with your order number. We'll guide you the rest of the way.

Are sale or discounted items returnable? +

Yes, with one small exception — items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. This will always be clearly labelled at the time of purchase.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it? +

Yes — within 12 hours of placing your order. After that, our atelier has likely begun preparing the piece. Email us as soon as possible and we'll do our best.

How do I track my order? +

You'll receive a tracking link by email and SMS the moment your order ships. You can also see live status from your Oormii account at any time.

What does "anti-tarnish" actually mean? +

Every Oormii piece is sealed with a thin rhodium layer. Rhodium is non-reactive — meaning your jewellery stays luminous through humidity, perfume, and time. With basic care, you'll see no tarnishing for years.

Is the jewellery hypoallergenic? +

Yes. All our pieces are 925 sterling silver and nickel-free, suitable for most sensitive skin. If you've reacted to other costume jewellery in the past, you should be safe with Oormii.

Do you offer custom or bridal pieces? +

Yes. We work on a small number of custom pieces every month. Email hello@oormiijewels.in with your idea and timeline; we'll be in touch within 48 hours with a sketch and quote.

How do I care for my Oormii jewellery? +

Three rules: (1) the last thing on, the first thing off; (2) avoid contact with perfume, lotion, and chlorine; (3) store in the velvet pouch we send with your order. The cleaning cloth in the box handles the rest.

Can I shower or swim wearing my pieces? +

You can shower briefly without harm, but please remove before swimming — pool chemicals and salt water will, over time, dull the shine. A few minutes in plain water is fine; a full afternoon in the pool is not.

What payment methods do you accept? +

All major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, popular wallets, and Cash-on-Delivery (for orders below ₹3,000). All payments are processed securely through Razorpay with bank-grade encryption.

Is my payment information secure? +

Absolutely. We never store card details on our servers. All transactions are PCI-DSS compliant and routed directly to our payment partner Razorpay.

Do you offer EMI? +

Yes — for orders above ₹3,000, EMI options on most major credit cards are available at checkout, including no-cost EMI on select banks.

Still have questions?

We answer every message — usually within 12 hours. Reach out by email, phone, or WhatsApp.
